- CONTROLS:

	* Q: Ghost invisible.

	* W: Ghost sniper.

	* R: Pause the game.

	* Spacebar: centers the cam to the selected unit.

	* Tab: Centers the camera between units.

	* Arrows: Move the camera.

	* Select Medic, click with right button another friendly unit: Medic will heal it.
